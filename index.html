Fostering Collaboration...

The University of Melbourne's Digital Health Journal Club aims to bring together researchers and PhD students in the field of digital health, which is a multidisciplinary field that involves the study and application of digital technologies to improve health outcomes, healthcare services, and overall well-being.

In these fortnightly journal club sessions, we aim to foster discussions, share insights, and stay updated with research. We will explore a variety of important, foundational, and influential papers, providing attendees a comprehensive understanding of a dynamic and evolving area.
