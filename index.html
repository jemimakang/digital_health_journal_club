Fostering Collaboration...

The University of MelbourneU’s Digital Health Journal Club aims to bring together researchers and PhD students in the field of digital health, which is a multidisciplinary field that involves the study and application of digital technologies to improve health outcomes, healthcare services, and overall well-being. You can find more information here if you are interested in learning more about what we do.

In these fortnightly journal club sessions, we aim to foster discussions, share insights, and stay updated with research. We will explore a variety of important, foundational, and influential papers, providing attendees a comprehensive understanding of a dynamic and evolving area.
